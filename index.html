Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find an answer, ask us today

  • What is Fegco Health?

    Fegco Health is Emergency response company that helps connect people with emergency needs to appropriate emergency responders such as ground private ambulance, hospital ambulance and air ambulance.

  • What is the website for?

    The website is to make it easy for those who need faster repsonse to different emrgencies such as an accient or heart attack etc.

  • Can I access to nearby hospital in rela-time?

    Yes. The website allows you to enter yor location and then the google map will immediately bring up the operational hospital nearby which you can then choose your preference.

  • Is your service expensive?

    Our services are relatively cheap for hospital and free for members.

  • Where is your office located?

    We have offices in different cities in africa but our headquarter is in Accra, Ghana along winter street.
